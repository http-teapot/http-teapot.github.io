What equipment do I need?

Most classes require no equipment. However, a yoga mat, towel, and waterbottle are always recomended. For all strength-related classes kettlebells or dumbbells are highly recommended.

Do I have to sign a contract?

We ask all members to sign a waiver of liability, but no other contract or agreement is needed to take class with us.

Do passes have different expiration dates?

Yes, all drop-ins expire 14 days after purchase, and 10-pack classes expire 5 weeks from the date of first pass used. On-demand 1-day passes expire 24-hours from purchase, and 1-week on-demand classes expire 7 days from day of purchase.
